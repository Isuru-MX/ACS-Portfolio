My Hobbies

Playing Music

Music has always been one of my greatest passions—a world I can escape into, where every note and chord lets me tap into something deeper. Whenever I pick up my guitar or sit down at the keyboard, I feel this sense of calm mixed with excitement, like I’m stepping into a space where time slows down and everything else fades away. Playing music is not just about producing sound; it’s a creative journey that allows me to express emotions that are often hard to put into words. Each session is a blend of experimentation and discovery, whether I’m trying out new chords, improvising melodies, or figuring out how to convey a particular feeling through sound.

What I love most is the challenge that music brings—it demands focus, patience, and a certain level of precision, but at the same time, there’s room for raw creativity and personal expression. In a way, it reminds me of my work in tech, where precision and creativity also go hand in hand. But with music, there’s an added element of freedom; it’s just me and the instrument, creating something purely for the joy of it. Over time, music has taught me the value of persistence, as each new skill takes time and practice to master. And in those moments when a melody finally comes together or a rhythm feels just right, there’s a sense of accomplishment that’s hard to beat. Playing music is a hobby I hope to carry with me for a lifetime—a space to recharge, create, and find joy in the simplicity of sound.

Playing Football

One of my absolute passions is playing football. There’s nothing quite like the thrill of stepping onto the field, feeling the rush of adrenaline as the game begins, and experiencing that pure sense of freedom that comes with being fully immersed in each moment. Football, for me, isn’t just a hobby—it’s a true escape, a way to reset, and a chance to completely recharge. I love the challenge it brings, the strategy it demands, and the intense focus it requires. There’s something uniquely satisfying about the camaraderie of working with teammates, building an unspoken connection on the field, and pushing each other to give it everything we have for the shared goal of victory. Every match reminds me why I love the game so much: it embodies resilience, teamwork, and the relentless drive to improve, to keep moving, and to push past limits. Football brings out a fierce determination in me, a sense of focus that I take with me into other areas of my life as well. No matter what’s happening in life or how busy things get, there’s nothing that re-centers me quite like a game of football. It’s the ultimate way for me to feel fully alive, to recalibrate, and to come back with a renewed energy and readiness to tackle any challenge that comes my way.
