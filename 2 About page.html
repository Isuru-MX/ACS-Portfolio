About Me

My name is Isuru Maduranga, a 30-year-old backend developer currently based in Moratuwa. I completed my schooling at Royal College, Colombo, and went on to graduate with a degree in Computer Science from the University of Westminster, through the Informatics Institute of Technology (IIT). With over eight years of experience in the IT industry, I have had the privilege of working at notable companies, including WSO2, and am now contributing my expertise at IFS. In addition to my role as a backend developer, I am an entrepreneur with my own businesses, where I continue to apply my skills in technology and innovation. Outside of work, I enjoy singing, playing music, and engaging in football, which bring balance and joy to my leisure time.
